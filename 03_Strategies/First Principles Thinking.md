# Strategy: First Principles Thinking

## Essence
Break problems down to fundamental truths, reason upward from physics or economics instead of relying on analogy.

## Key Mental Models
[[Systems Thinking]] · [[Comparative Advantage]] · [[Probability]]

## Playbook
1. Define the objective and constraints without legacy assumptions.
2. Decompose the problem into atomic components.
3. Reassemble based on fundamentals, then compare to existing solutions.
