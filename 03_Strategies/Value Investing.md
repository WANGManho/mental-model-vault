# Strategy: Value Investing

## Essence
Buy assets below intrinsic value with a margin of safety based on conservative estimates of future cash flows.

## Key Mental Models
[[Margin of Safety]] · [[Opportunity Cost]] · [[Probability]]

## Playbook
1. Estimate intrinsic value using discounted cash flow or comparable metrics.
2. Demand a significant discount to compensate for unknowns.
3. Monitor incentives and competitive dynamics such as [[Network Effects]].
