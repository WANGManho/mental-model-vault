# Decision: Sample Strategic Bet

Date: 2024-01-15

## Context
Considering whether to invest in building a proprietary data platform versus licensing an external vendor.

## Models Used
[[Opportunity Cost]] · [[Margin of Safety]] · [[Comparative Advantage]] · [[Loss Aversion]]

## Options Considered
1. Build in-house: high upfront cost, custom fit.
2. License vendor: faster deployment, potential lock-in.
3. Hybrid: start with vendor, gradually replace critical components.

## Decision
Pursue the hybrid approach to preserve optionality and validate assumptions before heavy capital expenditure.

## Outcome
In progress. Initial vendor deployment live in 90 days; parallel team prototyping unique analytics engine.

## Lessons Learned
- Documented how incentives between product and finance teams differed; neutral facilitator improved alignment.
- Next time, run pre-mortems to surface integration risks sooner.
