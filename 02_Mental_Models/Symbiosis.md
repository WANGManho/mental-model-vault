# Mental Model: Symbiosis

## Definition
Symbiosis describes long-term interactions between different species where at least one benefits; mutualism benefits both parties.

## Core Principle
Complementary strengths can create partnerships more resilient than solo actors, provided incentives remain aligned.

## When To Use
- Strategic alliances, ecosystems, and channel partnerships.
- Evaluating platform-versus-partner strategies.

## Examples
- Payment providers partnering with merchants to expand acceptance.
- Hardware companies bundling services with software integrators.

[[Incentives]] · [[Niche Construction]] · [[Comparative Advantage]]

## Opposite / Failure Cases
- Parasitic relationships where one side exploits the other.
- Situations with overlapping competencies causing competition.
