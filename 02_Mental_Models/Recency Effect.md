# Mental Model: Recency Effect

## Definition
The recency effect biases memory and judgment toward the most recent events or data points.

## Core Principle
Recent stimuli remain at the top of working memory, so they unduly influence evaluations, forecasts, and narratives.

## When To Use
- Performance reviews and KPI discussions.
- Incident response retrospectives where fresh pain overshadows longer trends.

## Examples
- Overreacting to a bad quarter despite multi-year growth.
- Forgetting earlier successes when rating an employee after a recent slip.

## Opposite / Failure Cases
- Dashboards showing long time series and rolling averages.
- Processes emphasizing base rates and historical context.
