# Mental Model: Entropy

## Definition
Entropy is the measure of disorder in a system; the second law of thermodynamics states that entropy tends to increase in closed systems.

## Core Principle
Without constant energy input, systems drift toward disorder, so maintenance and renewal are required to preserve structure.

## When To Use
- Evaluating operations, data quality, or culture maintenance.
- Understanding why checklists and automation are needed to prevent decay.

## Examples
- Codebases becoming brittle without refactoring.
- Teams losing alignment when rituals fade.

## Opposite / Failure Cases
- Systems with active feedback and energy inputs that maintain order.
- Chaotic creative processes where disorder is deliberately harnessed.
