# Mental Model: Inertia

## Definition
Inertia states that objects in motion stay in motion and objects at rest stay at rest unless acted upon by an external force.

## Core Principle
Momentum—physical or organizational—resists change, so altering course requires energy proportional to current velocity and mass.

## When To Use
- Assessing effort to change legacy processes or customer behavior.
- Planning reorgs, migrations, or lifestyle shifts.

## Examples
- Enterprise software entrenched by years of integrations.
- Habits persisting until friction or incentives shift strongly.

## Opposite / Failure Cases
- Highly volatile startups that pivot quickly due to low mass.
- Chaotic systems constantly disrupted by external shocks.
