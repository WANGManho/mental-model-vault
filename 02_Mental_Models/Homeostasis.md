# Mental Model: Homeostasis

## Definition
Homeostasis is the tendency of biological systems to regulate internal conditions to maintain stability despite external changes.

## Core Principle
Feedback mechanisms detect deviations and activate countermeasures, keeping temperature, pH, or other variables within safe ranges.

## When To Use
- Designing control systems, SLAs, or financial guardrails.
- Understanding how cultures maintain norms against disruptive shocks.

## Examples
- Thermostats adjusting HVAC output to maintain room temperature.
- Incident response teams restoring service baselines after outages.

## Opposite / Failure Cases
- Runaway positive loops without balancing feedback.
- Fragile systems lacking sensors or actuators to correct drift.
