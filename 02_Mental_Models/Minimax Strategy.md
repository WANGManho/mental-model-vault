# Mental Model: Minimax Strategy

## Definition
The minimax strategy chooses the option that minimizes the maximum possible loss, commonly used in adversarial or zero-sum games.

## Core Principle
When facing strategic opponents or high-stakes downside, prioritize robustness over average outcomes.

## When To Use
- Negotiations, cybersecurity, or competitive bidding where adversaries adapt.
- Planning for worst-case regulatory or litigation scenarios.

## Examples
- Allocating defenses to protect the most catastrophic failure mode.
- Setting conservative contract terms to cap liability.
