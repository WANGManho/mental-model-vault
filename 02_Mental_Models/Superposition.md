# Mental Model: Superposition

## Definition
Superposition in quantum mechanics means a system can exist in multiple states simultaneously until measurement collapses it.

## Core Principle
Keeping options open preserves flexibility; once you commit, you collapse alternatives, so delay irreversible choices until information improves.

## When To Use
- Product discovery keeping divergent solutions alive before selection.
- Strategy processes running parallel experiments before scaling.

## Examples
- Running multiple prototypes before locking a roadmap path.
- Venture studios incubating varied ideas before funding one spin-out.

## Opposite / Failure Cases
- Decisions with high cost of delay where early commitment captures scarce opportunities.
- Situations requiring focus where too many options create paralysis.
