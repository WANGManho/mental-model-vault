# Vault Guide

This vault guides you through building a personal Mental Model Knowledge System that merges Munger's latticework, Luhmann's Zettelkasten, and Obsidian's graph.

## Components
- **Capture**: quick notes, references, and fleeting thoughts land in `00_System` before they become permanent notes.
- **Zettel**: every new idea becomes its own atomic note with a unique title, clear purpose, and links to related notes.
- **Latticework**: map mental models across disciplines to find reinforcing or conflicting relationships.

## Recommended Workflow
1. **Capture** inspiration using a scratch note or the Daily Note plugin.
2. **Process** the note within 24 hours: decide whether it is a Principle, Mental Model, Strategy, Bias, Case, or Decision.
3. **Create** a permanent note using the relevant template inside the correct folder.
4. **Link** the note to related models, underlying principles, and applicable cases.
5. **Review** the new note via the local graph to ensure it connects to at least three other nodes.
