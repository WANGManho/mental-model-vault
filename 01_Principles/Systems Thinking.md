# Principle: Systems Thinking

## Essence
View problems as interconnected systems with stocks, flows, and feedback loops rather than isolated events.

## Why It Matters
- Prevents local optimizations that create global failures.
- Encourages building [[Mental Models Map|lattice connections]] between disciplines.

## Signals to Apply
- Hidden delays, unintended consequences, reinforcing or balancing loops.
