# Principle: Compounding

## Essence
Small advantages that reinvest into themselves create exponential growth or decay.

## Why It Matters
- Explains flywheel effects like the [[Amazon Flywheel]].
- Encourages patience in strategies such as [[Long-term Thinking]].

## Signals to Apply
- Reinvestment of gains, feedback loops, retention metrics.
