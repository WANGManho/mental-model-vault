# Principle: Probability

## Essence
Probability quantifies uncertainty and enables calibrated expectations about outcomes.

## Why It Matters
- Drives expected value calculations behind [[Opportunity Cost]] and [[Margin of Safety]].
- Forces explicit assumptions when weighing choices in [[06_Decisions|Decision Logs]].

## Signals to Apply
- Multiple possible outcomes with asymmetric payoffs.
- Need to compare mutually exclusive options.
