# Cognitive Bias: Sunk Cost Fallacy

## Description
The sunk cost fallacy is the urge to continue an endeavor because of resources already invested, even when future prospects worsen.

## Impact
- Traps teams in doomed projects rather than exploring higher-value opportunities.
- Masks the true [[Opportunity Cost]] of persistence.

## Countermeasures
- Focus on incremental expected value, not past spend.
- Use pre-defined review gates with kill criteria.
