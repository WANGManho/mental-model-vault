# Cognitive Bias: Availability Bias

## Description
We judge frequency or probability by how easily examples come to mind, not by actual base rates.

## Impact
- Overemphasizes vivid anecdotes over data.
- Causes reactive decisions after recent events, ignoring larger trends.

## Countermeasures
- Consult datasets and base-rate statistics alongside stories.
- Keep a library of [[05_Cases|case notes]] to compare anecdotes systematically.
