# Cognitive Bias: Loss Aversion

## Description
Loss aversion is the tendency to prefer avoiding losses over acquiring equivalent gains; losses loom larger in our psychology.

## Impact
- Leads to holding underperforming assets to avoid realizing losses.
- Encourages overly conservative choices that ignore [[Opportunity Cost]].

## Countermeasures
- Frame decisions in expected value terms with [[Probability]].
- Pre-commit to thresholds and use checklists.
