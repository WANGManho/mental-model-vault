# Case: Coca-Cola Brand Moat

## Snapshot
Coca-Cola leverages brand equity, distribution, and pricing power to sustain high returns on capital.

## Mental Models
[[Path Dependence]] · [[Network Effects]] (indirect via channel loyalty) · [[Incentives]]

## Strategic Insights
- Decades of marketing created emotional resonance that deters switching.
- Bottler agreements align incentives and ensure shelf dominance.

## Decision Lessons
- Invest early in brand-building assets that competitors cannot easily replicate.
- Use [[Margin of Safety]] when valuing intangibles—assess durability of the moat.
