# Case: Costco Pricing Strategy

## Snapshot
Costco caps markups, uses membership fees, and focuses on limited SKUs to deliver extreme value while maintaining profitability.

## Mental Models
[[Incentives]] · [[Comparative Advantage]] · [[Margin of Safety]]

## Strategic Insights
- Aligning pricing with member value builds trust and loyalty loops.
- Bulk purchasing reinforces [[Comparative Advantage]] in supplier negotiations.

## Decision Lessons
- Consider trade-offs between variety and operational simplicity.
- Transparent pricing can become a differentiating commitment device.
