# Case: Apple Ecosystem

## Snapshot
Apple integrates hardware, software, and services to create a seamless user experience that reinforces switching costs.

## Mental Models
[[Network Effects]] · [[Path Dependence]] · [[Systems Thinking]]

## Strategic Insights
- Tight integration improves reliability and creates default choices for consumers.
- Complementary devices and services (Watch, AirPods, iCloud) deepen lock-in.

## Decision Lessons
- Owning critical touchpoints can amplify [[Long-term Thinking]] strategies.
- Prioritize user experience loops that keep customers inside the ecosystem.
